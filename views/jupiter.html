Jupiter Facts: Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system, and it can hold 11 earths inside of it. Jupiter also has the shortest days with each day only being 9 hours and 55 minutes long. Lastly, Jupiter has 67 moons that orbit around it, and one of those moons, Ganymede, is the largest moon in the solar system.
